Drone Weather Forecast

This Privacy Policy describes how Renan Rafael Bertoldo ("we", "us", or "the developer") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the mobile application Drone Weather Forecast (package identifier xyz.droneweather.app), the website droneweather.xyz, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms, please do not use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and a unique account identifier from the chosen provider. Sign-in is optional for most features but required to post in the community forum and to purchase or restore the Pro upgrade on iOS.
  • User-generated content. Posts, comments, and replies you publish in the forum, together with the display name associated with your account.
  • Support communications. Messages you send to us by email.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location data. With your permission, the app uses your device's GPS to provide weather forecasts for your current position. You may also enter a location manually; in that case no GPS data is collected. Location data is processed in real time to query weather providers and is not stored on our servers.
  • Device motion and orientation. Used locally on your device to power the compass feature. This data never leaves your device.
  • Device and usage data. Anonymous identifiers, app version, operating system version, language, country, crash reports, and aggregated usage events (such as which screens are opened).
  • Push notification tokens. If you enable notifications, we store the device token provided by Apple Push Notification service or Firebase Cloud Messaging so we can deliver alerts.
  • Purchase records. When you make an in-app purchase on iOS, we store the validated receipt identifier and entitlement state (e.g., "Pro Lifetime active") associated with your account. We do not receive or store your credit card or payment details — those are handled exclusively by Apple.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide weather forecasts, compass, wind profile, safe-zone maps, and other in-app features.
  • To authenticate you and synchronize your settings and entitlements across devices.
  • To operate the community forum, including moderation of abusive or unlawful content.
  • To validate in-app purchases and restore entitlements (iOS only).
  • To send push notifications you have opted in to receive.
  • To diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve the Service through aggregated analytics.
  • To prevent fraud and abuse via app integrity checks (Firebase App Check, DeviceCheck/App Attest).
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / LGPD)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Brazil, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance — to provide the features you request, including in-app purchases.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the Service secure, prevent abuse, and improve features.
  • Consent — for optional features such as precise location and push notifications. You can withdraw consent at any time in your device settings.
  • Legal obligation — when we must retain records to comply with tax, consumer, or law-enforcement requirements.

5. Data Storage and Security

Account data, forum content, and entitlement records are stored on Google Cloud / Firebase infrastructure, primarily in data centers located in the United States. We use TLS for all network communication and rely on Firebase security rules and Firebase App Check to restrict access to authenticated, legitimate clients.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

  • Account profile and entitlements are retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Forum posts and comments remain published until you delete them or your account.
  • Crash logs and anonymous analytics are retained for up to 14 months and then aggregated or deleted.
  • Purchase receipts are retained for the period required by Apple's developer agreement and applicable tax law.

10. In-App Purchases

On iOS, the app offers a one-time non-consumable purchase (pro_lifetime_ios) that unlocks premium features, as well as optional consumable "tip jar" purchases that support development. All payments are processed by Apple. We receive a validated receipt from Apple that contains the product identifier, a transaction identifier, and the purchase date — we do not receive your payment method, card number, or billing address.

On Android, the app is free and offers only optional donations processed by Google Play.
